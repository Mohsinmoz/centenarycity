about us

Eagle Hills

Eagle Hills is a global developer of iconic real estate assets and a provider of premium lifestyles that helps countries raise their global profiles to new heights. Our properties aim to become flagship city destinations that invigorate nations.

Backed by unprecedented credentials in creating mixed-use masterplanned communities, we build residential projects that create a lifestyle capturing the very best the city has to offer. With the pedigree as a developer of global mega-projects, we have led urban expansion and renewal projects that positively impact business and tourism while creating a ‘halo effect’ that ensures sustainable economic growth.

Founded in the United Arab Emirates, our company is now looking to share its expertise with aspiring nations throughout the world.
