NIGERIA HERITAGE PARK

The wonder of nature in daily life

Nigeria Heritage Park will offer a picturesque outdoor venue in the heart of nature, a destination to be enjoyed by Centenary City's nearby residents and workers. It also boasts an abundance of open green areas for social gatherings where families can have an energetic day of fun in the sun or a relaxing picnic under a shady tree. Wide-open spaces and scenic views add to the appeal of events and entertainment for everyone's enjoyment.
