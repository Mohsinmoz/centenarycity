A LUXURIOUS
DESTINATION

The Address Abuja offers serviced villas and apartments as well as executive and presidential suites spanning across 25 hectares of the finest in world-class living with luxurious amenities and fine restaurants.

LEISURE IN
THE HEART
OF NATURE

World-class golfing, the 18-hole international golf course forms a majestic landscape, offering picturesque views and pleasant surroundings for the nearby residents.

BRANDS OF
THE WORLD

The Mall of Nigeria gives residents access to an extensive range of international brands, complete with abundant entertainment options and dining outlets.

AFRICA
TOWER

At the core of Centenary City stands it's most magnificent centrepiece, Africa Tower. Set to be the highest of its kind in Africa, the tower will house 30 floors of Abuja's most luxurious apartments and 46 floors of premium offices.

INTERNATIONAL
COMMERCIAL
CENTRE

The International Commercial Centre (ICC) will play host to Africa's booming businesses. With 120,000m2 of prime office space, ICC will form the perfect environment for businesses to thrive in a community atmosphere.
